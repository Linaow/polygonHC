The Orange Cat Processor

Engineered for maximum chaos output despite minimal core count.

Technical Specifications

Cores: 1 (running purely on vibes, no logic gates detected)

RAM: Whatever's left on the kitchen counter

Battery Life: 18 hours a day (in sleep mode)

Operating Temperature: Only functions properly in direct sunlight

Known Issues

Randomly sits in boxes 2 sizes too small for no logical reason. Refuses to acknowledge new furniture for 3–5 business days before approving it. Occasionally screams at 3am for reasons the manufacturer cannot explain.

Why Only One Core?

Field studies (i.e. every cat owner ever) confirm that additional cores would not improve performance. The orange cat's single-core architecture is optimized entirely for knocking objects off tables, one decision at a time.
